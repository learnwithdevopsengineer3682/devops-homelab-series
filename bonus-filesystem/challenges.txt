Bonus Filesystem Challenges
--------------------------
1. Read the last line of your system log (/var/log/syslog or /var/log/messages).
2. Use 'find' to list all .conf files in /etc.
3. Change permissions of a test file with chmod, then restore them.
4. Find which directory uses the most space in your home directory.
5. Share your favorite Linux filesystem command or tip by opening an Issue or commenting on YouTube!
